Overview

I decided to create my Kentucky Dams in Relation to Water Bodies and Urban Areas map because I have an interest for Kentucky's watersheds. Kentucky has the most navigable water bodies second only to Alaska. There are many maps created to show and research Kentucky's watersheds so I decided to map dams in relation to urban areas and water bodies/streams. From this map you can see where dams are in relation to larger cities and you can see what water bodies they are near or may be utilizing.
